About Us

The Novel Nanophotonic Phenomena team, part of the Photonics & Nanotechnology Group in the Physics Department at King's College London, conducts both theoretical and experimental research. Our focus includes ultrafast routing of light based on spin–orbit interactions in nanophotonics, polarization analysis and synthesis using integrated nanostructures, and studying novel optical forces for manipulating nanoparticles, molecules, or single atoms near nanomaterial surfaces.

We are committed to collaborative innovation, bridging disciplines to transform groundbreaking concepts into real-world applications.
